# Accounts Receivable User Guide

## Overview
Manage customers, create invoices, and track payments.

## Managing Customers

### Add Customer
1. **AR > Customers > Add Customer**
2. Enter: Code, Name, Email, Phone, Credit Limit
3. **Save**

## Creating Invoices

### New Invoice
1. **AR > Invoices > Add Invoice**
2. Select customer
3. Enter: Date, Due Date
4. Add line items (Description, Qty, Price)
5. **Save**

### Send Invoice
1. Open invoice
2. Click **Send**
3. Email sent to customer

## Recording Payments

### Record Payment
1. **AR > Payments > Record Payment**
2. Select customer and invoice
3. Enter: Amount, Date, Method
4. **Record**

### Partial Payments
- Enter amount less than invoice total
- Invoice status: **Partially Paid**
- Remaining balance tracked

## Collections

### Aging Report
**AR > Reports > Aging Report**
- View overdue invoices
- Contact customers for payment

### Payment Reminders
1. **AR > Collections**
2. Select overdue invoices
3. Click **Send Reminders**

## Reports

### Customer Statement
**AR > Reports > Customer Statement**
- Select customer and date range
- View all transactions
- Email to customer

### Revenue Report
**AR > Reports > Revenue**
- View revenue by period
- Filter by customer/product

## Tips
- Send invoices promptly
- Follow up on overdue payments
- Offer payment plans when needed
- Review aging report daily
