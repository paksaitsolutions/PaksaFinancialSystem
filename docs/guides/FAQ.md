# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General

### What is Paksa Financial System?
Enterprise-grade financial management platform for managing accounting, payroll, inventory, and more.

### What browsers are supported?
- Chrome (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge

### How do I reset my password?
1. Click **Forgot Password** on login page
2. Enter email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password

## Accounts Payable

### How do I add a vendor?
**AP > Vendors > Add Vendor** - Enter vendor details and save.

### Can I edit a bill after approval?
No. Approved bills cannot be edited. Create a credit memo to adjust.

### How do I process batch payments?
**AP > Batch Payments** - Select multiple bills, set payment details, process.

### What payment methods are supported?
Check, Bank Transfer, ACH, Wire, Credit Card

## Accounts Receivable

### How do I create an invoice?
**AR > Invoices > Add Invoice** - Select customer, add line items, save.

### Can I send invoices via email?
Yes. Open invoice and click **Send** to email customer.

### How do I record partial payments?
Enter amount less than invoice total when recording payment.

### How do I handle bad debt?
Create credit memo or write-off entry in General Ledger.

## General Ledger

### How do I create a journal entry?
**GL > Journal Entries > Add Entry** - Enter debits and credits (must balance).

### What is a trial balance?
Report showing all account balances to verify debits equal credits.

### How do I close a period?
**GL > Period Close** - Review, adjust, then close period.

### Can I reverse a journal entry?
Yes. Open entry and click **Reverse**.

## Payroll

### How do I add an employee?
**Payroll > Employees > Add Employee** - Enter employee details and compensation.

### How do I process payroll?
**Payroll > Pay Runs > Create Pay Run** - Select period, review, process.

### Can I edit a processed pay run?
No. Contact administrator to reverse and reprocess.

### How do I generate payslips?
Automatically generated when pay run is processed.

## Reports

### How do I export reports?
Click **Export** button and choose Excel or PDF.

### Can I schedule reports?
Yes. Click **Schedule** and set frequency and recipients.

### How do I customize reports?
Use filters and date ranges to customize data shown.
